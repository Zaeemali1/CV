I am a QA at CitrusBits Inc. My research focuses on the interaction of softwares Quality and Bug free, and the roles they play in large-scale social and information systems. My work has been supported by an NSF Career Award, an ONR Young Investigator Award, a MacArthur Foundation Fellowship , a Packard Foundation Fellowship , a Simons Investigator Award , a Sloan Foundation Fellowship , and grants from Facebook, Google, Yahoo, the MacArthur Foundation, the ARO, and the NSF. I am a member of the National Academy of Sciences, the National Academy of Engineering, and the American Academy of Arts and Sciences.
